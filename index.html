Welcoming to Laundry Wallah/Laundry Mart.

Laundry refers to the process of cleaning clothes, linens, and other textiles to remove dirt, stains, and odors. It typically involves washing (by hand or machine), rinsing, drying (air-drying or using a dryer), and often ironing or folding. Beyond just cleanliness, laundry helps maintain fabric quality, extend the life of garments, and ensure hygiene.
